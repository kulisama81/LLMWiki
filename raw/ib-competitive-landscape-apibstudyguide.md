### IB 2025 Syllabus Coverage Gaps

**IB Biology HL** — Currently covered: C1.2 Cell Respiration, C1.3 Photosynthesis. Missing: A1 Unity & Diversity, A2 Continuity & Change, A3 Interaction & Interdependence, A4 Form & Function, B1-B3 (Nucleic Acids, Proteins, Carbs & Lipids), C1.1 Enzymes, D1-D4 (Cell Biology, Meiosis, Neural Signaling, Heart/Kidney/Muscle).

**IB Math AA HL** — Currently covered: Complex Numbers. Missing: sequences/series/binomial/proof by induction, functions, geometry/trig, statistics/probability, calculus (differentiation, integration, differential equations, Maclaurin series).

**IB Chemistry SL** — Currently covered: Reactivity 3 Organic Chemistry. Missing: Structure 1-3, Reactivity 1-2.

## Part 3: Reddit & Discord Posting Strategy

### Timing
Ideal window: April 14-28 (2-4 weeks before May 2026 exams). Post Tuesday or Wednesday evenings UTC. Space posts 5-7 days apart.

### Rules for Engagement
- Never open with a link — lead with value, site at the end
- Reply to every comment within first 2 hours (early engagement is main ranking lever)
- r/IBO will downvote anything that reads as self-promotion in first sentence

### Post 1: "Study Tips" flair (higher engagement)
"5 things I wish I knew before the IB Bio HL exam (Paper 2 edition)"
- Confusing light-dependent vs. light-independent reactions
- Writing "enzyme changes shape" instead of "active site changes shape"
- Misidentifying organelles in electron micrographs (ribosomes vs. glycogen granules)
- Getting meiosis vs. mitosis mixed up on genetics questions
- Under-explaining gas exchange (need to name carbonic anhydrase and bicarbonate conversion)

### Post 2: "Resources" flair
Direct resource announcement with subject-by-subject links. Emphasizes: 2025 syllabus, inline diagrams, exam alert boxes, free PDF downloads, no account needed.

### Discord Strategy
Post in #resources channels. Lead with value, mention site as supplementary.
